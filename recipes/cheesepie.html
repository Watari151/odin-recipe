THAT'S HOW YOU MAKE A CHEESE PIE

All the ingredients we're gonna need:

  • 300g Wheat flour
  • 125g butter
  • 2 tablespoon water
  • 3 eggs
  • 300ml semi-skimmed milk
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 pinch peppers
  • 150g Grated Arla Lactofree Cheddar
  • 300g salad mix

Directions:

  1. Rub the flour and butter together to make a crumbly mixture. Add the water and work quickly together to form a dough. Roll out the dough for an oven tin 15 x 35 cm, or a round tin about 24 cm in diameter.
  2. Let rest in the refrigerator for about 30 minutes. Heat the oven to 200 Â°.
  3. Par-bake the pie case for about 10 minutes. Beat together eggs, milk, salt and pepper. Add the cheese and mix well.
  4. Pour the filling into the pie case and bake in the middle of the oven for about 25 minutes. Serve the pie with salad.
